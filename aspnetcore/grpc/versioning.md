## <a name="backwards-compatibility"></a><span data-ttu-id="75d6f-109">下位互換性</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="75d6f-109">Backwards compatibility</span></span>

* <span data-ttu-id="75d6f-121">**新しいサービスの追加**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="75d6f-121">**Adding a new service**</span></span>
